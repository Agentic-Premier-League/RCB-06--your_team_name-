Interview Tips

Use the STAR Method

Structure answers as Situation, Task, Action, Result to keep responses clear and impactful.

Think Aloud

Narrate your thought process — interviewers value your reasoning, not just the final answer.

Clarify Before Diving In

Ask clarifying questions upfront. It shows thoroughness and prevents solving the wrong problem.

Practice Daily

Even 15 minutes a day builds significant confidence over 2–3 weeks.
